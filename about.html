Our Story

Discover the passion behind Travel Buddy PR

Who We Are

Founded in 2015, Travel Buddy PR began as a small team of travel enthusiasts with a shared vision: to transform ordinary trips into extraordinary experiences. What started as a passion project has grown into Puerto Rico's premier travel companion service.

We're not just another travel agency - we're your personal connection to authentic, unforgettable adventures. Our team of local experts knows every hidden gem, from secluded beaches to the best local eateries.

Our Mission

To connect travelers with the soul of Puerto Rico through personalized, authentic experiences that go beyond typical tourism.

We believe travel should be transformative, not transactional. That's why we focus on creating meaningful connections between visitors and local culture, nature, and communities.

Every itinerary we create is designed to showcase the real Puerto Rico - vibrant, diverse, and endlessly fascinating.
